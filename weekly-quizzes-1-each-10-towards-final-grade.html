BIOSCI 220: Quantitative Biology

Weekly quizzes (1% each, 10% towards final grade)

There are eleven (11) weekly quizzes due at the end of each week from week 2 - week 12 (due Friday at 10pm). These are worth 1% each, where your one (1) quiz that has the lowest mark will be dropped from your final grade. This means overall, your weekly quizzes will total 10% towards your final grade. These are designed for you to solidify concepts and practice skills taught each week and thereby keep up with the material. There are practice quizzes for you to attempt prior to sitting the weekly quiz, these are not assessed. There is also a practice quiz for week 1.

We are looking forward to meeting you and working with you all this semester.
